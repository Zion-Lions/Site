About Us

We are a group of 4 come from EPITA, a french computer engineering school. This game is a project for the second semester and therefore lasts about 6 months. We're currently developping a First Person Shooter based on a futurist history. The game is programmed in C# with the framework Unity.

Weapons

In this game you'll be able to play with a lot of weapons like the famous M4A1, or with a gun.

Mazes

In this game, you'll go in different environments. You'll visit some dark mazes where you'll meet hostile enemies...

Enemies

In the mazes, you will meet enemies who look like human beings, they are Cosmic Humanoids .

Modes

You will be able to go through different modes, for example : Multiplayer, Survival, or Normal. In Multiplayer mode you will be able to fight against your best friends (or not), in Survival Mode, you will have to survive against spawning enemies on a special map. Finally, in Normal Mode, you will fight against Artifical Intelligences and you will have to pass all the mazes to win. Have a good game !
